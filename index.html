About

Proyek kolaborasi ini memanfaatkan limbah kaleng karena material ini menawarkan solusi berkelanjutan, mendorong inovasi kreatif, memiliki nilai ekonomi melalui potensi daur ulang, dan berkontribusi pada edukasi masyarakat tentang pentingnya pengelolaan sampah yang bertanggung jawab.

Observation of Used Cans

Tin waste is an inorganic type of garbage that is difficult to decompose and has the potential to pollute the environment, but it also has potential for utilization through reuse and recycling into value-added products.
