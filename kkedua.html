Observation Of Can Waste

Waste is the residue from an activity or production process that is no longer used. There are many types of waste that can be utilized, one of which is cans. In this observation, I will explain about can waste.

A can is a container or vessel shaped like a tube or cylinder, usually made of thin metal (such as aluminum or tin), and is used to store various products including food, beverages, or non-food items. Can waste is commonly found in the surrounding environment, especially in garbage disposal areas, small shops, and food or beverage industries.

Can waste is categorized as inorganic waste that is difficult to decompose naturally. Although it is hard to break down, can waste can be reused through recycling processes or transformed into more useful items. By utilizing it, the volume of waste can be reduced and it can also have economic value.

Based on the results of the observation, can waste belongs to inorganic waste that is difficult to decompose. However, we can reuse can waste to make pencil holders, flower pots, or vases. The utilization of can waste not only helps maintain environmental cleanliness but also increases creativity and provides economic benefits for the community.
